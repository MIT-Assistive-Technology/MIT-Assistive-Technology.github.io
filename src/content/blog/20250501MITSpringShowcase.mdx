## The Final Showcase

**Celebrating innovation, collaboration, and accessibility at our Spring 2025 showcase!**

This semester, our teams have been hard at work developing assistive technology solutions alongside our incredible codesigners. From mobility aids to communication tools, each project represents months of collaborative design, iterative development, and meaningful impact.

![](/src/assets/images/atspring25-29.jpg)

Our end-of-semester final showcase brought together students, codesigners, faculty, and community members to celebrate these incredible projects. The event highlighted not just the technical achievements, but the collaborative relationships and real-world impact that make AT\@MIT special.

Each team presented their work through detailed posters and live demonstrations, showcasing both the engineering process and the human stories behind each project!
