## Fall 2025 Final Showcase

**Ending the Fall 2025 semester by showcasing new projects, new partnerships, and continued momentum toward accessible, open design!**

This semester, AT\@MIT teams are launching a new slate of assistive technology projects developed in close collaboration with our codesigners and community partners. From mobility and communication tools to accessible gaming and daily-living supports, each project is grounded in rapid iteration, hands-on prototyping, and sustained codesign.

In particular, we're excited to share a new partnership with MIT-Ukraine, which has led to the formation of our Adapted Game Controller team.

![](/src/assets/images/IMG_9408.jpeg)

Fall 2025 represents an important step toward scaling impact beyond MIT while preserving the deeply collaborative, human-centered process that defines AT\@MIT.
