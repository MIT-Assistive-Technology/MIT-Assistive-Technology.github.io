## Join Us for the Spring 2026 Final Showcase!

**Friday, May 8, 2026**  
**4:00 PM - 6:00 PM**  
**Charles Vest Student Street, Stata Center (Stata Lobby)**

AT @ MIT works with codesigners from the Greater Boston Area to address real problems they face in day to day life. Combining the expertise of our students and co-designers, we iteratively design and test innovative solutions. Come meet our teams and see their projects in action!

## Featured Projects

- One-Handed Game Controller
- Adaptive Gaming Team
- Cane Stabilizer
- Blind Assistance - Guided Crosswalk Navigation
- Speech to LaTeX
- Open-Source Sports Wheelchair Innovation
- Wheelchair Tray Modification
- Rollator Modification

## Event Details

- **When:** Friday, May 8, 2026, 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM
- **Where:** Charles Vest Student Street, Stata Center (Building 32)
- **Who:** Open to the public—all are welcome!
- **What to Expect:** Live demonstrations, project presentations, and opportunities to meet our teams and codesigners

We look forward to seeing you there as we celebrate another semester of collaborative innovation in assistive technology!

For questions or more information, email us at <a href="mailto:atmit@mit.edu">assistivetech@mit.edu</a>.
